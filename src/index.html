About us

TogetherintheUK was set up as new, innovative social enterprise in January, 2015. Its purpose is to help people immigrating to the UK to live a good life. It has 3 objectives:

  • A space to share stories

  • A space where you can find useful tips and more information

  • A space to expand your network and find events

  • To find some wonderful stories: go to: http:togetherintheUK.net
