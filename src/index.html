TogetherintheUK

A social enterprise which brings together people who have come to live in the UK with others, through story telling.

About us

TogetherintheUK was set up as new, innovative social enterprise in January, 2015. Its purpose is to help people immigrating to the UK to live a good life. It has 3 objectives:

  • A space to share stories

  • A space where you can find useful tips and more information

  • A space to expand your network and find events

To find some wonderful stories go to: togetherintheuk.net

To find out about events and join in the discussion: visit our Facebook page, find us on Twitter as @TogetherintheUk or contact us at via email on tgiuk@outlook.com
